Apart from the mouse, the keyboard can be used:


(slowed with Shift):

Right, Left, Fore, Back:
  F S E D
Up, Down, Time+, Time-:
  R W T G
Zoom:
  + -

Rotate (modifies 2 axes each):
  (RF)	Left Right
  (RU)	PageUp PageDown
  (RT)	U J
  (FU)	Up Down
  (FT)	I K
  (UT)	O L


(inversed with Shift):

Increase Maximum Number of Iterations:
  N
Increase Number Precision:
  P
